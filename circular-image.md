# Photoshop Circular Image

1. Open Photoshop
2. Drag the image to it
3. Click on the image layer to select it 
    - `f7` will open the layers window
4. Open `Eliptical Marquee Tool` by pressing `u`
5. Move the mouse over the image to a starting point
6. Press and hold `shift`, click, and drag to create a uniform circle
7. Release the mouse when done
